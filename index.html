🟩 Afterpay — should you use it?

Afterpay lets customers split orders into 4 fortnightly payments, interest-free. It's hugely popular in Australia and can increase average order size — especially for bulk buys of $100–$300. We recommend adding it — many halal household buyers shop in bulk and would benefit from spreading the cost. Setup is via the Afterpay merchant portal and takes a few days to approve.

🔵 Zip Pay — should you use it?

Zip Pay works similarly but allows up to $1,000 credit with more flexible repayment. It suits larger orders (e.g. bulk wholesale). Worth considering once you have regular bulk customers. Can be added alongside Afterpay with no conflict. Both integrate into most payment processors (Stripe, Square, etc.).
